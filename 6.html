Top Tourist Attractions

Let SafarSathii take you to Dakshina Kannada's most beautiful destinations.

🛕 Spiritual

Kateel Durgaparameshwari Temple

Kateel Durgaparameshwari Temple is one of Dakshina Kannada's most iconic spiritual spots, built right on a small island in the middle of the River Nandini. The flowing water around the temple creates a peaceful, almost magical vibe as soon as you enter. It's famous for powerful Devi worship and attracts devotees throughout the year.

📍 Kateel, Dakshina Kannada
🌿 Eco-Tourism

Pilikula Nisargadhama

Pilikula Nisargadhama is a massive eco-tourism and educational park that offers a full-day escape into nature. Spread across a zoo, botanical garden, lake, heritage village, and a science centre, it's perfect for families and students alike. The boating on the calm lake is a favourite activity, and with greenery everywhere, it's one of the most complete recreational spots near Mangalore.

📍 Pilikula, Mangalore
🏖️ Beach

Panambur Beach

Panambur Beach is known as one of Karnataka's cleanest, safest, and best-maintained beaches. It's famous for water sports like jet skiing, banana rides, and parasailing. The beach hosts kite festivals and cultural shows, and the sunsets here are stunning — making it a hotspot for photographers and evening walkers alike.

📍 Panambur, Karnataka
